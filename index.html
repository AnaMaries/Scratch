Our Story

They say every girl dreams of her wedding day. At Ana Marie's, we've always dreamed of helping you find the look that makes you say to yourself, "I feel like a bride." Over ten years ago, Ana Marie's opened, making our dream come true through helping hundreds of brides realize their dream wedding day look.

Come in, help us get to know you and we'll match you with your ideal gown - whether you'll begin married life in a cathedral, a great hall, on the beach or on a grand lawn - you'll find your dream's match at Ana Marie's.
