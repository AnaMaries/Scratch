Jasmine Collection

Jasmine Bridal has provided ease of mind and satisfaction for thousands of customers. Our customization options for our unique bridal dresses make creating the gown you've been dreaming about possible.

Jasmine Curve offers bridal gowns that start at size 20 to 34 with modest adjustments to create a comfortable fit. The all-new Jasmine Curve bridal collection is designed with full-figured brides in mind while focusing on appropriate bust support and shoulder coverage with raised necklines, wider straps, and more. In addition, this collection offers the same sleek designs without sacrificing style. Jasmine Curve is also available in regular sizes for brides who desire a more modest look as well.

Jasmine Bridal recognizes the importance of reflecting individual style and personality in every dress. This is why our bridal consultants are dedicated to assisting each bride in finding the perfect balance between traditional elegance and personal flair. Our extensive range of fabrics, colors, and embellishments ensures that every bride can add a unique touch to her gown, making it truly one-of-a-kind. At Jasmine Bridal, we believe that every bride deserves to shine on her special day, and our commitment to quality and individuality makes this possible.


Designer Dreams at Ana Marie's Bridal

At Ana Marie's Bridal, we specialize in a unique white-labeling system, partnering with emerging design houses to offer designer-quality wedding gowns without the designer price tag. Our carefully curated collection showcases the luxury and elegance of high-end fashion, making your dream dress attainable and affordable. This approach not only supports new talent in the bridal industry but also ensures our brides enjoy a diverse, trendsetting selection.

Our boutique in Jefferson City stands out for its blend of style, quality, and value. We bypass traditional markups of big brands, focusing instead on direct sourcing from smaller designers. This means every bride can experience the joy of wearing a stunning, high-fashion gown that fits both her individual taste and budget. At Ana Marie's Bridal, it's not just about the dress; it's about making your special day memorable and accessible.
